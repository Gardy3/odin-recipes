Choc Chip Cookies

Description

Choc chip cookies are delicious. They not only feed the tummy but they also feed the soul.

Ingerdients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Choc Chips

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Roll mixture into little balls and place on tray.
  3. Put in oven to cook.
  4. Eat them... EAT THEM ALL!
